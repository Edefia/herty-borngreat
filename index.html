About Herty

Gospel Music Icon

Herty Borngreat is an award-winning gospel artist whose journey began with her debut album "Odo Ben Ni" in 2010. With multiple awards including Best New Artist and Best Contemporary Gospel at VGMA, she continues to inspire millions through her powerful music and unwavering faith.
